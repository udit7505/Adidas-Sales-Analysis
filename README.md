This Power BI project provides an in-depth analysis of Adidas sales data, featuring interactive dashboards that
visualize key metrics such as revenue trends, product performance, and customer demographics. 
The analysis includes:
Sales Dashboard: Visualize total sales, revenue trends, and performance over time.
Product Analysis: Breakdown of sales by product categories, brands, and individual items.
Geographical Analysis: Sales distribution and performance across different regions and locations.
Interactive Visuals: Utilize interactive charts, maps, and filters for in-depth exploration.
